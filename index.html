Racer's Cycle Service was founded in 2001 to supply Utah County with quality bicycle repair and racing expertise. Racer Gibson has worked as mechanic for World Tour Road and Pro Mountain Teams, traveling the NORBA and World Cup circuit, Winning World cups and a National Championship. He has been a mechanic at MTB World Championships and more recently at The Tour of Utah.

Racer is an early member of the PBMA, the Professional Bicycle Mechanics Association, and is a PBMA Certified Mechanic

As an athlete, in 2003 Racer won the 30+ Short Track National Championship, was on the AMTRI 24 Hour Singlespeed World Championship Team, and held an XC Pro Racing license. Racer's goal in business is to give this same level of commitment to your bicycle, and help you achieve your own goals.

During the winter Racer spends his days away from the shop giving sled dog rides with his pack of Huskies. Team G Sled Dogs
